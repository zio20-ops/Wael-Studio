Mockups are realistic visual representations used to showcase designs in a practical, real-world context. They allow designers to present their work—such as logos, branding, packaging, websites, or apps—on objects like business cards, billboards, devices, or products. Mockups help clients and audiences better understand how a design will look when applied, making presentations more engaging, professional, and convincing. They are essential tools for previewing, testing, and marketing design concepts before final production.

The Free Design category highlights creative works developed without strict constraints or client requirements, allowing designers to fully express their ideas, style, and imagination. It includes experimental concepts, personal projects, and artistic explorations that showcase originality, innovation, and technical skills. This category reflects a designer’s unique vision and passion, often pushing creative boundaries beyond commercial limitations.

The Social Media Design category focuses on visually engaging content created specifically for digital platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn. It includes posts, stories, banners, and ad creatives designed to capture attention, communicate messages effectively, and strengthen brand identity. These designs combine creativity with strategy, using layout, typography, color, and trends to boost engagement, increase reach, and deliver impactful visual communication in fast-paced online environments.

The Teachers Posters Design category focuses on educational and informative visuals created to support teaching and enhance learning environments. These posters combine clear communication with engaging design, presenting concepts, instructions, or motivational messages in a visually appealing way. Designed for classrooms, workshops, and academic settings, they aim to simplify information, capture students’ attention, and create an inspiring educational atmosphere through effective use of layout, typography, and color.

Projects category showcases a diverse collection of design works that do not fall under a specific category. It includes a variety of creative projects that highlight versatility, problem-solving skills, and adaptability across different design styles and purposes. This category serves as a general portfolio space where designers present their work, demonstrate their capabilities, and explore multiple creative directions.
